Dell Mechanical Contracting is a locally owned and operated company in Thunder Bay, servicing Northwestern Ontario. Driven by our dedication to excellent quality workmanship and service, Dell Mechanical specializes in complete packages for residential projects: One stop shopping for home builders when it comes to septic fields, site services, in-floor heat, boilers and plumbing. Other services include plumbing for new construction, renovations and repairs of residential and commercial projects.

The Dell team brings practical and working knowledge with local code requirements, suppliers and long standing relationships with other trades and sub-trades which are integral to the success of each project.

Services

It is our firm commitment to maintain the highest quality of workmanship, attracting, developing and retaining the best people, thereby exceeding customer expectations at a reasonable cost. We recognize that on-time delivery is paramount for our customers.

Dell Mechanical works with the Owner and General Contractor throughout the project to provide value engineering solutions, giving the customer the most efficient and effective system upon completion.
